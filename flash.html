How it works
01
PICK YOUR BOARD
Select your ESP32 variant — WROOM, C3, or S3. Each one gets its own pre-compiled firmware binary tailored to that chip's exact architecture and memory layout.
02
SET YOUR HOTSPOT
Choose your own WiFi name and password. No code editing required — your values are embedded into the firmware automatically before it gets written to your chip.
03
CONNECT & ENJOY
Click Flash. Chrome opens a port selector. Select your board and ESP Web Tools handles everything — erase, write, verify. Under 60 seconds. Then connect to your WiFi and go to 192.168.4.1.
